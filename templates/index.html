What is Phishing and How We Help You Stay Safe

Phishing is a sneaky trick that bad people on the internet use to try and steal your personal information. They might send fake emails or messages, or create websites that look real, but are actually designed to fool you into giving away things like your password or bank account details. Sometimes, these fake messages even hide harmful files called malware, which can damage your computer or steal your data.
